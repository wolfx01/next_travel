===========================================================
PROJECT PRESENTATION: TRAVEL - GLOBAL TRAVEL COMPANION
===========================================================

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
-------------------
• Name: Travel
• Slogan: "Discover the Best Destinations in the World"
• Purpose: A smart, all-in-one global travel platform designed to help explorers discover destinations worldwide, plan trips using AI, and connect with a global community of travelers.

2. PROBLEM & SOLUTION
---------------------
• Problem: Travelers often juggle multiple apps to find international recommendations, plan complex cross-border itineraries, and connect with like-minded explorers.
• Solution: "Travel" provides a unified platform for discovering countries and cities globally, generating AI-powered itineraries for any destination, and building a worldwide social network.

3. KEY FEATURES
---------------
A. Global Discovery
   • World Map & Countries: Explore destinations across different continents (e.g., France, Japan, Brazil, Morocco).
   • Places Gallery: Filter by Country, City, or Category (Beach, Cultural, Mountain, Historical).
   • Smart Search: Instantly find countries, cities, specific attractions, or other travelers.
   • Rich details: Comprehensive information, weather updates, and maps for locations worldwide.

B. AI Integration (Gemini Powered)
   • AI Trip Planner: Generates personalized, day-by-day itineraries for ANY international destination based on user preferences.
   • Dynamic Descriptions: AI-generated insights for places to ensure up-to-date and engaging content.

C. Social & Community
   • Global Profile: Customizable profiles to showcase your international travel history.
   • Social Stats: Track "Saved Places", "Followers", and "Reviews Written".
   • Profile Rating System: A trust-building system where travelers rate each other (1-5 stars).
   • Activity Feed: See where travelers from around the world are visiting.
   • Real-time Chat: Connect with travelers from different cultures and countries.
   • Follow System: Build your global network.

D. Booking & Tools
   • Aviasales Integration: Built-in flight booking widget for international flights.
   • Authentication: Secure Login/Register with a premium, user-friendly interface.

4. TECHNOLOGY STACK
-------------------
• Frontend: Next.js 15 (App Router), React, Vanilla CSS (Premium Custom Styles).
• Backend: Next.js API Routes, Node.js.
• Database: MongoDB (via Mongoose).
• AI Model: Google Gemini.
• Authentication: Custom JWT Auth.

6. CONCLUSION
-------------
"Travel" is the ultimate companion for the modern globetrotter, combining the power of AI with a social community to make exploring the world accessible and exciting.
